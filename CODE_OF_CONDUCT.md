# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of Frosty, are committed to creating an open,
welcoming, and respectful environment for everyone. We pledge to make participation
in this project a harassment-free experience, regardless of age, body size,
visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity
and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality,
personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We aim to foster a community where people feel safe to contribute, learn, and collaborate.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and considerate in communication
- Welcoming newcomers and helping others learn
- Giving constructive feedback and accepting it gracefully
- Taking responsibility for mistakes and learning from them
- Focusing on collaboration and improving the project

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary behavior of any kind
- Offensive comments related to personal identity or background
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory remarks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any behavior that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Enforcing this Code of Conduct fairly and consistently
- Reviewing and addressing reported issues
- Taking appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues,
or other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

- GitHub repositories (issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Communication channels related to the project
- Any public or private interaction where an individual represents Frosty

---

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers.

All reports will be handled confidentially and reviewed as soon as possible.

Maintainers will take appropriate actions depending on the severity of the issue,
which may include:

- Warning the contributor
- Removing content
- Temporary or permanent ban from the project

---

## Guidelines for Contributors

To help maintain a healthy community:

- Be clear and respectful when opening issues or pull requests
- Avoid aggressive or dismissive language
- Assume good intent from others
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project
- Be patient — maintainers and contributors may have limited time
